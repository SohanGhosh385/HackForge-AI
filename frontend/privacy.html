Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 26, 2026

At HackForge AI, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit and interact with our website.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require user account registration, so we do not collect personal identifiers like your name, address, or phone number. We only collect the following types of information to provide and improve the service:

  • Parameters & Form Inputs: We collect the domain names, skill levels, and available hacking hours you input into the Ideas Generator tool. These inputs are used strictly to generate the project ideas and are not linked to any personal identity.
  • Technical & Usage Data: Like most websites, we may collect technical metadata, such as your IP address, browser type, device type, operating system version, and general usage statistics (e.g. pages visited, duration of visit) through standard server logs and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To process your parameter inputs and generate calibrated hackathon project proposals.
  • To monitor, analyze, and optimize the performance and speed of our prompt engine and backend system.
  • To troubleshoot connection failures and maintain application stability.

3. Cookies and Tracking

HackForge AI does not use tracking cookies for advertising or behavior profiling. We may use session-based storage or local cookies strictly to preserve your technical parameters or local states (such as active panel toggles or light/dark preferences) during your session.

4. Third-Party Services & APIs

Our application interfaces with third-party Large Language Model (LLM) providers to generate ideas, scoring metrics, and checklist roadmaps. Any prompt inputs we forward to these service providers are completely anonymized and do not contain personal data. These third-party APIs process data in accordance with their respective developer privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard administrative, physical, and technical security measures designed to protect the integrity of our platform. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for developers and students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@hackforge.ai.
